RESIDENTIAL RENT AGREEMENT

This Rent Agreement is made on this 1st day of April, 2026, between the Landlord (Party A) and the Tenant (Party B).
WHEREAS the Landlord is the absolute and lawful owner of the premises, and the Tenant has requested to take the said property on rent.

4. MAINTENANCE OBLIGATIONS: The Tenant agrees to completely repaint the entire premises (interior and exterior) at their own absolute cost every 11 months before the renewal of this agreement.

7. LATE PAYMENT PENALTY: The Tenant absolutely agrees to pay a strict penalty of 5% of the total monthly rent for every single day the rent is delayed past the 5th of the month.

The tenant shall maintain the premises in good and tenantable condition (subject to normal wear and tear).

12. TERMINATION AND EVICTION: The Landlord reserves the absolute right to terminate this agreement and evict the Tenant within 24 hours of verbal notice, for any reason whatsoever, and may lock the premises without judicial intervention.

Signed by both parties in presence of respective witnesses.
